# Code of Conduct

Ormedian Labs is committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone.

## Our standards
We expect participants to:
- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent; ask clarifying questions
- Focus on evidence, reproducibility, and clear communication

We do not tolerate harassment, discrimination, or abusive behaviour.

## Enforcement
If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, contact: hello@ormedian.com.

This document is adapted from the Contributor Covenant principles.
